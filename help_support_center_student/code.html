AI Ethics & Guidelines

The Human-First Policy

Cognitive Sanctuary is designed to augment, not replace, your thinking. Use AI to generate outlines, explain concepts, and debug code—but always provide the final creative spark.

Integrity Protocols

Never submit AI-generated text as your own original work. Use our built-in citations tool to transparently acknowledge when AI assisted in your research process.

Secure Token Vault

Your data is isolated and encrypted using enterprise-grade Auth0 protocols. No third-party AI training ever uses your personal academic data.

Master the Art of Prompting

1

Provide Rich Context

Tell the AI your grade level, the specific assignment goals, and the tone you want to achieve.

2

Iterative Refinement

Treat the AI like a study partner. Ask it to "Explain that like I'm 10" or "Now give me a counter-argument."

3

Define Constraints

Ask for "bullet points only" or "limit the explanation to 100 words" to get concise answers.

Example Interaction

"I'm writing a paper on the French Revolution. Can you help me find three primary causes but explain them as if we're in a history debate?"

AI Mentor Insight

"Challenge accepted! Let's examine the Socio-Economic divide, the Enlightenment ideals, and the Fiscal crisis from a 1789 perspective..."

Frequently Asked Questions

Is using this cheating?

Not if used correctly! Our tools focus on explaining 'how' and 'why', empowering you to do the actual work yourself.

Can teachers see my data?

Privacy is our priority. Your interactions are private unless you choose to export your 'Evidence of Learning' reports for credit.

Does it cite sources?

Yes! Every major factual claim includes a 'Source Check' that links to academic journals and verified databases.

Still have questions?

Our team of educators and AI ethicists are available 24/7 to help you navigate your digital learning journey.
